<p><strong>8<sup>th</sup> Speech and Prize-Giving Day</strong>
</p>
<p>Our school's annual Speech Day was a vibrant and memorable celebration,
bringing together staff, students and parents to honour the achievements
of our students. The event was filled with pride and joy as we recognised
the hard work, dedication and success of our students across various domains.</p>
<p>The event featured a series of captivating performances that showcased
the diverse talents within our school community. The school band opened
the event with an energetic performance, followed by the melodious sounds
of the Guzheng ensemble. The Modern and Ethnic dance groups lit up the
stage with their graceful and dynamic routines, while the school choir
delivered a heartfelt and harmonious performance. Each act reflected the
passions and commitment of our students.</p>
<p>This special day not only celebrated academic excellence but also, the
spirit of unity and creativity that defines our school. It was truly a
day to remember for all who attended.</p>
