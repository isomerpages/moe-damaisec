<p><strong>Graduation Day 2025</strong>
</p>
<p>On 26 September 2025, the Secondary 4 and 5 cohorts gathered in the school
hall for their much-anticipated Graduation Day. The event marked both a
celebration of achievements and closure of an important chapter in their
journey at Damai Secondary School.</p>
<p>The hall was filled with joy and anticipation as students, together with
their form teachers, subject teachers, school leaders, and the Year Head,
came together for the ceremony. Words of encouragement were shared by the
peer support leaders from each class, each moment symbolising the culmination
of years of effort, growth and resilience. The recipients of the Most Outstanding
Student award from each class received their awards from Ms Chan, our principal.
Smiles and laughter were abundant during the video montage segment as memories
of shared struggles and triumphs filled the air.</p>
<p>After the ceremony, students had fun at the photo booth which captured
snapshots of friendships and bonds that would be cherished for years to
come. The celebration continued with a buffet lunch, where conversations
flowed easily and heartfelt thanks were exchanged. It was a fitting finale
to the students’ secondary school journey — a day of gratitude, joy and
hope as the Class of 2025 looks forward to new horizons.</p>
