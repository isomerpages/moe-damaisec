<p><strong>Hari Raya Aidilfitri Celebration</strong>
</p>
<p>On 21 April 2025, our school came alive with festive joy as students and
teachers gathered for a vibrant Hari Raya Aidilfitri celebration during
the assembly. Emceed by our very own sec 4 students, the assembly programme
featured lively quizzes, vlogging showcases, musical performances, and
a fashion parade highlighting traditional Malay attire. Teachers, Ms Wirda
and Ms Umairah, charmed the crowd with their own rendition of “Selamat
Hari Raya,” while students joined in a spirited sing-along. Interactive
elements such as the ketupat showcase and cultural presentations deepened
the audience’s appreciation for the celebration. It was a heartwarming
event that united the school community in festive spirit and cultural pride.</p>
