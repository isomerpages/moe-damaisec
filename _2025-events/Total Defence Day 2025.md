<p><strong>Total Defence Day: Food Resilience Preparedness Programme</strong>
</p>
<p>On Total Defence Day, students stepped into the shoes of a nation facing
a food supply crisis during the Food Resilience Preparedness Programme.</p>
<p>Instead of heading to the canteen for a usual recess, students gathered
in the hall to share a communal meal. Served in compact boxes and eaten
at room temperature, the Ready-to-Eat meals included dishes such as Chicken
Bolognese Pasta, Curry Chicken with Briyani Rice, Fish Porridge with Sweet
Potato and Pumpkin, Sweet Spicy Tomato Fish with Basmati Rice and even
a vegetarian Marinara Pasta option.</p>
<p>To complete the experience, the canteen offered a simplified lunch menu
to reflect the limited ingredients that would be available during a food
disruption.</p>
<p>This meaningful exercise left a lasting impression. It reminded students
that food security cannot be taken for granted, and that resilience is
not just about planning but also, staying adaptable, even at mealtimes.</p>
