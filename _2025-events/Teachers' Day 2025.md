<p><strong>Teachers’ Day 2025: My Teacher, My Hero</strong>
</p>
<p>This year, Damai Secondary celebrated Teachers’ Day with the theme “My
Teacher, My Hero.” To honour our teachers, every staff member received
a customised superhero tote bag, a symbol of their strength, dedication
and the inspiration they bring into students’ lives. Alongside this, students
contributed to a Thank You Journal and wrote heartfelt cards, each message
a reminder of the lasting impact teachers have on their journeys.</p>
<p>The celebration continued with a concert consisting of student-initiated
performances that filled the hall with music, energy and creativity. The
grand highlight was the finals of the Damai Spotlight competition, where
the acoustic band, <em>The Serenades</em>, featuring Magarett, Benneth,
Issac and Jayden, took the top prize with their soulful performance.</p>
<p>In a touching moment, the staff came together for a cake-cutting ceremony,
celebrating the heroes who shape lives daily with patience and passion.</p>
<p>The second part of the day saw students and teachers retreat to their
classrooms for class parties, filled with laughter, games and joyful bonding.</p>
<p>During the staff lunch, our teachers enjoyed a Netflix-inspired afternoon
of fun, mystery and friendly competition. Teachers arrived dressed as iconic
characters from their favourite shows, from Squid Game to Emerald Hill.
It was truly a celebration worth streaming.</p>
