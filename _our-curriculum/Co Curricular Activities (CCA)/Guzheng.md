<p><strong><u>Guzheng Ensemble</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The Damai Guzheng Ensemble aims to develop students with good character,
strong core values and a love for the Chinese classical music.&nbsp;Students
undergo training sessions conducted by a professional instructor.</p>
<p>Through the weekly practice sessions and numerous performances together,
the Ensemble aims to develop strong teamwork, responsibility and confidence
in students.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Students are given opportunities to gain exposure and enhance their confidence
through school and/or outdoor performances.</p>
