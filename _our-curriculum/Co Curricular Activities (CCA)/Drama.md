<p>The Damai Drama Club nurtures a love for theatre and empowers students
to express themselves with confidence and creativity.</p>
<p>Through drama, our members learn to see the world from different perspectives,
developing empathy, teamwork and self-awareness. We believe that drama
not only enriches the mind but also, feeds the soul. We aim to help students
grow as expressive, thoughtful and compassionate individuals.</p>
<p>Each week, members engage in hands-on sessions that build acting techniques,
stage presence and essential life skills such as collaboration, decision-making
and communication. Here, creativity thrives, and every unique voice has
a place on our stage.</p>
