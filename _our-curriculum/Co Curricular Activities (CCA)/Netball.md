<p>The Damai Netball Team provides a rigorous and holistic training programme
designed to empower our student athletes to reach their fullest potential.
More than just building netball skills, the programme develops discipline,
resilience and teamwork — qualities that prepare our students to excel
both on and off the court.</p>
<p>Under the guidance of Coach Ariel, training sessions are thoughtfully
customised to strengthen tactical awareness, technical ability and physical
fitness. At the same time, students are challenged to step up as leaders,
taking ownership of their growth and supporting their teammates in pursuit
of shared goals.</p>
<p>Our team also participates in a variety of competitions, giving athletes
the platform to sharpen their skills, apply strategies under pressure and
gain invaluable competitive experience. Through these opportunities, students
not only grow as confident players but also, as leaders who inspire and
uplift those around them.</p>
<p><strong>Signature Programmes</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Leadership Training Programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Division Leaders Development, Sports Camp, friendly competitions with
teachers, Organising National Day Carnival game</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participation in external carnivals and friendly competitions with schools</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>SPEED Carnival, friendly competition with Springfield Secondary School</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Volunteering for External Events</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>House-to-House Care Pack Distribution &amp; Elderly Befriending @ Kampong
Chai Chee</p>
</li>
</ul>
