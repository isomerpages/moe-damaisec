<p><strong><u>Introduction</u></strong>
<br>For the safety of our students and staff, a traffic management plan has
been established to identify safe routes for pedestrians and vehicles.
We seek everyone’s cooperation to adhere to the traffic management plan
for the safety of the school community.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/traffic.png">
</div>
<p><strong><u>Vehicle<br></u></strong>We seek drivers’ understanding to adhere
to the following safety instructions:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Observe speed limit of&nbsp;<strong>15km/h</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Slow down</strong>&nbsp;at round-about</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Adhere</strong>&nbsp;to Road Marshals’ instructions at all times</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure that the passenger alights or enters the vehicle from the&nbsp;<strong>LEFT</strong>&nbsp;side
of vehicle only</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Only <strong>LEFT</strong> turns are permitted when exiting the school premises
for safety reasons</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><u>Pedestrian<br></u></strong>All pedestrians are reminded to
adhere to the following safety instructions:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Adhere</strong>&nbsp;to Road Marshals’ instructions at all times</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Watch out for moving vehicles even if you have the right of way</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Wait for all vehicles to come to a complete stop before crossing the road</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Refrain</strong>&nbsp;from listening to music while walking to
school</p>
</li>
</ul>
