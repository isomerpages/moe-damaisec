<h4>The LEAP Award 2026</h4>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="LEAP Award 2026" src="/images/Information/LEAP_Award_2026_TN.jpg">
</div>
<p><strong>The</strong>  <strong>Listening Educator for Advancement and Progress (LEAP) Award</strong>  <strong>recognises</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>exceptional teachers who have made a difference in the lives of their pupils.</strong>
</p>
<p>Open to ALL in-service teachers.</p>
<p>Nominees for the awards should fulfill the following criteria:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Passionate in teaching, nurture students from the heart</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create positive impact on students’ character development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use innovative and effective methods to motivate students and make a difference
in their lives</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Nominees must be Singapore citizens</p>
</li>
</ul>
