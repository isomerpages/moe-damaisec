<p><strong>RELEASE OF 2025 GCE O-LEVEL EXAMINATION RESULTS</strong>
</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;The results of the 2025 Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate
of Education Ordinary Level (GCE O-Level) examination will be released
on <strong>14 January 2026, 2.00 pm</strong>.</p>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students should report to the school hall by 1.30 pm for a briefing on
admission exercises and registration matters before the official results
release at 2.00 pm.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are strongly encouraged to wear their school uniform.&nbsp; If
they choose not to come in their school uniform, they must be appropriately
dressed i.e. no skimpy or revealing clothing or no attire with offensive
words or graphics, and no slippers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents/guardians are invited to accompany their child/ward to the school
to collect the GCE O-Level results. If using their own vehicles, parents/guardians
should park in the car parks at the HDB blocks next to the school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>School candidates who are unable to collect their results in person can
appoint a proxy to collect a physical copy of their results on their behalf
from the school. Proxies are required to produce the relevant documents
for the schoolâ€™s verification when collecting the results. School candidates
may contact their schools for assistance if they are unable to appoint
a proxy.</p>
</li>
</ol>
