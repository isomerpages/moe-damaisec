<p><strong>Parents Support Group (PSG) @ Damai</strong>
</p>
<p>Parents play a key role in the growth and development of their children.
As key stakeholders, parents’ partnership with the school is essential
in supporting students’ learning and well-being. PSG@Damai embraces the
school’s vision and mission, and actively supports the school’s endeavours.
Our PSG members role model the school values, add value to students’ school
experiences, and serve as a bridge between the school and the wider parent
community.</p>
<p><strong>Our Key Roles</strong>
</p>
<p>The PSG supports and collaborates with the school in the following ways:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>serving as chaperones during learning journeys and school events</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>participating actively in school activities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>organising and conducting family activities to strengthen familial bonds
and build meaningful relationships</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>oromoting parenting skills through workshops and sharing sessions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>serving as resource persons to support school programmes</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Our Logo</strong>
</p>
<p>The <strong>PSG@Damai logo</strong>, designed by members in 2021, reflects
the spirit of partnership. The <em>heart of the flower</em> symbolises the
PSG’s dedication and commitment. The petals outlined by the word <em>Damai</em> represent
the strengthening of the PSG–school partnership to enhance students’ learning
experiences. The five petals depict the five key roles of the PSG.</p>

<p><strong>Parenting Resources</strong>
</p>
<p>Parents can access a range of parenting resources to support their child’s
development:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong><a href="https://familiesforlife.sg/pages/Article/Parenting-Programmes?utm_source=google&amp;utm_medium=sem&amp;utm_campaign=fflparenting-sem&amp;gad_source=1&amp;gclid=Cj0KCQjwsJO4BhDoARIsADDv4vCvvXlQoR312hUYy8yRzHtAqTbO4CTEmm_P1br4K_dkmSDZSzjhePcaApboEALw_wcB" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Positive Parenting Programme (Triple P)</a></strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Triple P is an evidence-based programme that equips parents with practical
strategies to promote their children’s psychological, social and emotional
well-being. It offers different levels of support to meet parents’ varying
needs.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/parentkit" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE Parent Kit</a>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>PSG Highlights 2025</strong>
</p>
<p>In 2025, PSG@Damai organised two bonding workshops for parents and their
children:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>The 5 Love Languages Workshop</strong> — conducted by a parent
volunteer. This workshop helped parents connect more deeply with their
children by understanding their unique love languages.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Parent–Teen Sneaker Art Jamming Session</strong> — a fun and creative
activity designed to strengthen parent–child relationships and create lasting
memories.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition, the PSG actively contributes to major school events such
as Secondary 1 Camp, National Day, Racial Harmony Day and Teachers’ Day,
enhancing the vibrancy and sense of community at Damai.</p>
