<p>It was with great pleasure that we welcomed Minister Chan Chun Sing to
our school in April. He spent the time getting to know our students and
staff. The day started off with a visit to the classrooms where he gained
some insights into our curriculum. His entrance into the Sec 1 Music class,
Sec 2 Mathematics lesson and Sec 3 English period brought a flurry of excitement
for the students. It is not everyday that you get a Minister for a ‘classmate’.
Both staff and students enjoyed the dialogue sessions with Minister Chan
as he proved to be witty and easy-going, putting all at ease. Moreover,
he took the time to get to know us on a personal level and addressed our
queries well. Minister Chan, thank you for visiting us and we look forward
to another visit from you!</p>
