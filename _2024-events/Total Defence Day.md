<p>Total Defence Day was commemorated with unique activities and educational
sessions. The day included food disruptions with no meat sold in the canteen,
emphasising resilience in scarcity. Cohort activities featured hands-on
experience such as first-aid demonstration, lashings and gadgets activity, <em>Guardians of the City</em> card
game and an IPP simulation exercise promoting strategic thinking and teamwork.
A talk by maritime industry professionals highlighted national security
perspectives. The day culminated with a Total Defence quiz, engaging students
in interactive learning about national preparedness. These events deepened
understanding and appreciation for Singapore's Total Defence among students
and staff.</p>
